1.1.20.1. Outline

 

This error may occur due to a communication error between the Main Board and the Servo Board, or a rapid change of motion. When a communication error occurs, a valid command cannot be transferred from the Main Board to the Servo Board, so this error will occur and immobilize the Robot to prevent the Robot's abnormal operation based on an invalid command.

Also this error will occur and immobilized the Robot because the drive unit may not follow the rapid changes of motion command.

(2)   Examine if there is a job program that operates a Robot rapidly

Please check if the error occurs at a point where the Robot's motion changes rapidly. If the error occurs during the rapid motion, modification of job program is required.

 

The reason that this error occurs during the rapid motion is as below. During the execution of job program, Robot's position may be twisted in order to move a short distance. At that point, the speed of Robot's axis suddenly increases, and the error may occur if the Servo Board follows the movement. In order to resolve this, please modify the Teaching point of the location (where the position changes rapidly) or make changes on the position of Robot.
